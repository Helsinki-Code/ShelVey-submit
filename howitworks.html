Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how ShelVey works and what makes it different.

Unlike traditional link building that relies on manual outreach or PBNs, ShelVey automates the entire content distribution process across 400+ platforms. We use a hybrid approach combining AI automation with human quality assurance for high-value sites. This gives you a much larger volume of backlinks with significantly less time investment, while maintaining quality standards that pure automation can't achieve.

ShelVey follows white hat SEO practices. We distribute valuable content to legitimate, indexed platforms—not spam sites. Our system ensures content quality and relevance, avoiding over-optimization and other practices that trigger penalties. The diversity of platforms creates a natural link profile, and our human QA team ensures submissions to high-DA sites meet each platform's guidelines. Our clients consistently see ranking improvements without penalties.

Our AI analyzes your content structure and key topics, then adapts it for each platform's specific requirements. This includes reformatting text to match character limits, optimizing headings and metadata, resizing and compressing images, generating platform-specific descriptions, and adding appropriate tags. The AI also identifies optimal anchor text placement for backlinks and ensures content maintains topical relevance while avoiding duplicate content penalties.

Most clients see initial results within 2-4 weeks, with significant improvements by months 3-6. The timeline depends on several factors: your starting Domain Authority, content quality, competition in your niche, and submission volume. You'll see immediate results in terms of backlink volume and diversity, while Domain Authority and organic traffic improvements typically follow a growth curve that accelerates over time as search engines index and value the new backlinks.

ShelVey works with almost any type of valuable content: blog posts, articles, infographics, PDFs, slide presentations, videos, and more. The best results come from informative, educational, or entertaining content with real value for readers. Thin, promotional, or low-quality content won't perform as well. Our system works particularly well with evergreen content that can be repurposed across multiple formats (like converting blog posts to PDFs or slide decks).
