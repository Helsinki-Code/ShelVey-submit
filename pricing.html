Why Professional is our most popular plan

The Professional plan hits the sweet spot for most businesses. It provides substantial platform coverage (250+ sites) while delivering critical features that significantly reduce manual work:

  • 15 human-assisted premium submissions to high-DA sites like LinkedIn (DA 98), Medium (DA 96), and other platforms that typically require manual handling

  • Full link management that automatically identifies and fixes broken links across all your distributed content

  • Content recycling & repurposing that transforms your original content into multiple formats for wider distribution

  • Image optimization that automatically compresses images and creates SEO-friendly alt tags

Our internal data shows Professional plan users see an average 28% higher ROI compared to Basic users, primarily due to the additional premium platforms and automated maintenance features.

When to choose Enterprise

The Enterprise plan is ideal for larger businesses and agencies that manage multiple websites or have high-volume content production needs. Consider upgrading to Enterprise if:

  • You're publishing more than 20 pieces of content monthly that need distribution

  • You need maximum platform coverage across all categories (directories, article sites, etc.)

  • You require a custom distribution strategy tailored to your specific industry and target audience

  • You prefer the dedicated support of an account manager who understands your business goals

  • You need full API access to integrate ShelVey with your existing content management system

Tip: Many agencies start with the Professional plan and upgrade to Enterprise once they reach 15+ clients. The account manager and custom strategy become essential at scale.
